================================================================================
INTERPRETATION
================================================================================

Truth-tellers show significantly MORE disfluency than liars. This is the opposite
of what 35% of the global population believes (GDRT, 2006). The mechanism is
cognitive: accessing real episodic memories in real-time generates retrieval
interference, self-monitoring, and temporal reconstruction effort — all of which
produce hesitations, false starts, and filler words. Liars who have rehearsed a
narrative do not experience this cognitive load and speak more fluently.

The public belief that disfluency indicates deception is not merely wrong — it is
inverted. The system penalises truthful speakers for the very cognitive effort
that makes their testimony authentic.

Binary Classification:
  Disfluency-only model accuracy: 60.3%
  (vs. human judgment: 54%, chance: 50%)
  A single linguistic variable outperforms trained human judges.
